CAD Productivity tools

Experience the evolution of design with the powerful new features of AutoCAD® 2015 software. AutoCAD now includes a refreshed modern interface and timesaving productivity tools such as the New Tab page, Ribbon galleries, and Command preview. The new interface has an updated look and feel that helps improve the design process. The darker interface is not only aesthetically pleasing, but it also reduces eyestrain by making fine lines, buttons, and text easier to see.

Speaker : Venkata Ramana

Venkata Ramana is Mechanical Engineering Graduate with Specialization in Production Engineering. Worked for Shield Machine Tools as Production Engineer to take care of Production schedules and production processes for various types of cutting blades and tools for different materials like Paper, Wood, Steel sheet etc., worked with an Solar Photo Voltaic Design Consultant, as Assistant Engineer to take care of marketing Survey on SPV awareness in the urban area of Hyderabad, to assist solar industry. Worked with Ultratech Engineers which is an SPM (Special Purpose Machine) Design and Manufacturing Industry as an ngineer, Involved in designing the various design elements of the machines, purchase schedules, production schedules and used CAD concepts extensively during 1997.Worked in Samhita in various levels from CAD Engineer to Center Manager, conducted training activates to student community, corporate trainings in CAD, Parametric, Visualization and Customization tools..
